The Formula (simple)

Weekly pay = £150 base
+ 10% of labour profit
+ monthly milestone bonus (see phases)
Labour profit = Revenue − Materials (with markup) − Contractor cost

Labour profit is the money left on a job after we pay the contractor and cover materials. You earn 10% of it — the harder you work the pipeline, the more you take home. No cap.
