# Handy Services — Contractor Working Agreement (Ad-hoc / per-job)

_Plain-English working terms. Version 1.0 — July 2026._

> ⚠️ **Before you rely on this: get 30 minutes with a solicitor/accountant.** This is
> drafted to keep the relationship a genuine self-employed B2B subcontract (IR35-safe),
> but the wording of the status clauses (§2, §7) should be checked before you sign anyone.
> This is the "papering" the council flagged — don't skip it.

---

## Who this is between

- **Us:** Handy Services (Handy Services Ltd / trading name), Nottingham.
- **You:** an independent, self-employed tradesperson running your own business.

This is **not** an employment contract, not a zero-hours contract, not a franchise, and
not exclusive. You keep your own clients and your own business. You're free to accept or
decline any job we offer, with no penalty.

---

## How the work works

1. **We offer you jobs.** Each job comes to you already quoted, scoped, and deposit-paid
   by the customer, with the **price you'll be paid attached before you accept.**
2. **You accept or decline.** Your call, every time. No obligation to take anything.
3. **You do the work** to a professional standard, supplying your own tools, transport,
   and materials unless we agree otherwise in writing.
4. **You sign the job off** with a completion photo and a short note.
5. **You invoice us. We pay you** (see "How you get paid" below).

You control **how** you do the work. We're the customer's point of contact; you're the
trade doing the job.

---

## How you get paid  *(the one boring, true sentence)*

- **Rate:** the per-job price shown on each job card, agreed **before** you accept. Ad-hoc
  work is paid **per job, not per hour** — do it well and fast, you keep the full job price.
- **You invoice us on the day you sign the job off.**
- **We pay by bank transfer within [1 working day] of a signed-off job.** No 30-day waits,
  no chasing.
- Your promotion rate once you reach Core: **£___ / day guaranteed**, plus you still earn
  the job price on top _(leave blank until papered + costed — do not quote this yet)_.

> _Interim note (internal): until Stripe payouts are wired, pay is by manual same-day/next-day
> bank transfer against the contractor's invoice. Destination = "100% via platform"; not
> a launch blocker._

---

## What we need from you before your first job  *(vetting gate — no docs, no job)*

You cannot be sent a paying job until **all** of these are on file:

- [ ] **Public liability insurance** — valid certificate, min £[1m/2m] cover. **Your policy
      covers your work** (insurance of record sits with you).
- [ ] **Right to work in the UK** — passport / share code / relevant document.
- [ ] **Proof of trade** — a couple of recent job photos, a reference, or a qualification.
- [ ] **Bank details** — for payment (account name, sort code, account number).
- [ ] **This agreement** — read and confirmed (a text back is fine to start).

---

## The audition → Core path  *(self-scored — you tick it, not us)*

Everyone starts on ad-hoc, per-job. You move up to **Core** — priority on jobs, first pick,
and a guaranteed day rate — when you've earned it against a checklist **you can see and tick
yourself**:

- [ ] **5 jobs completed**
- [ ] **On time** on all of them (arrived in the agreed window)
- [ ] **On quote** on all of them (no surprise overruns / callbacks)
- [ ] **Finish photo** submitted on each

Hit those and we have the Core conversation. The rule is fixed and visible — we're not
judging you on numbers you can't see.

**Staying ad-hoc is a valid choice.** If you just want good jobs, paid fast, on your own
terms — that's a complete deal on its own. Core is for people who want guaranteed days.

---

## The standards (kept short)

- **Turn up in the window**, or tell us early if you can't.
- **On the job, you represent Handy Services** — our polo (provided), our customer. Please
  don't hand out your own cards to *our* customers or take *our* customers direct. Your
  own clients are 100% yours; ours stay ours.
- **We handle the customer.** Complaints, awkward conversations, chasing money — that's us,
  not you. You do the work to standard and move on.
- **Mistakes:** if something's not right, we sort it with the customer. First honest
  mistake, we cover the cost and tell you the standard for next time. We only pass on cost
  if the same avoidable mistake repeats.

---

## Ending it

Either side can stop working together at any time, for any reason, with no notice and no
penalty. Any job already accepted gets finished and paid.
