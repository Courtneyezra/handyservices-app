## Scoring & Segmentation

Based on answers, segment landlords:

| Segment | Criteria | Tailored Offering |
|---------|----------|-------------------|
| **DIY Enthusiast** | Comfortable with tenant repairs, cost-conscious | Full self-repair program, max rewards |
| **Cautious Controller** | Nervous about tenant fixes, wants oversight | Photo verification, limited task list |
| **Hands-Off Investor** | Multiple properties, wants automation | Auto-approval, budget caps, minimal involvement |
| **Problem Property** | High callout rate, older buildings | Easy-fix installations priority, proactive maintenance |
| **New Landlord** | 1-3 properties, learning | Education-focused onboarding, guided setup |

## Implementation Notes

1. **Progressive disclosure** - Don't ask all at once, spread across onboarding
