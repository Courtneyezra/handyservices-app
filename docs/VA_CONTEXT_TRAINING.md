# VA Context Input Training Guide

## Why This Matters

The quote system uses AI to write a personalised quote page and WhatsApp message for each customer. The more context you give it about who the customer is and their situation, the better the quote will convert — better price, better copy, better CTA.

**A thin context input = generic quote = lower conversion.**
**A rich context input = quote that speaks directly to that customer = higher conversion.**

---

## How to Input Context

After speaking with a customer, open the **Generate Contextual Quote** page and find the **Customer Context** card.

You have two options:

1. **Type it** — write a few sentences in the text box
2. **Record it** — tap the microphone button and speak naturally (your voice is transcribed automatically)

The recording option is faster and captures more nuance. Speak like you're telling a colleague about the call.

---

## What to Include

You don't need to answer every question. Share whatever you know. The more the better.

### About the person
- Are they a landlord? Homeowner? Renter? Property manager?
- Do they own one property or many?
- Are they present at the property or remote?
- Are they older and cautious, or busy and want it quick?
- Did they mention a budget concern or were they happy to pay for quality?
- Were they stressed? Relaxed? In a rush?

### About the job
- What's the actual problem (not just what they said on the phone)?
- How long has it been going on?
- Is it urgent or can it wait?
- Have they had anyone else look at it already?
- Any access issues? Tenant involved? Key to collect?

### About the relationship
- Is this their first time calling us?
- Did they mention a recommendation or review?
- Did Ben speak to them or did you call back?

## Examples

### Thin context (❌ don't do this)
> Landlord. Tap dripping.

The system has almost nothing to work with. The quote will be generic.

---

### Good context (✓ aim for this)
> She's a landlord, lives about 2 hours away from the property. Tenant reported a dripping tap in the bathroom — she hasn't seen it herself. She was really keen on getting photos sent after the job so she knows it's done. Mentioned she's used us before and happy with the work. No urgency, tenant is fine for us to visit during the week.

**What the system does with this:** Writes a quote that speaks directly to remote landlords — emphasises photo report, tenant coordination, no-hassle logistics. CTA becomes "Sort My Rental."

---

### Rich context (✓ excellent — use voice recording)
> This is Mike, he's got a few properties in the area — sounds like a portfolio landlord. Called about a boiler service but mentioned his plumber let him down recently so he's looking to consolidate with one reliable company. He's very price-aware, mentioned "I just want a fair price for good work." He won't be at the property — tenant will let us in. Wants the invoice to go directly to him for tax. He asked about recurring maintenance contracts, I said we'd mention it after the first job.

**What the system does with this:** Prices with win rate in mind (price-sensitive signal), emphasises reliability and single-supplier value, keeps tone professional. Batch discount nudge included. No hard sell on the contract — that's post-job.

## Behavioural Checkboxes

Below the context box are three quick signal checkboxes. Tick any that apply:

| Checkbox | When to tick |
|---|---|
| **Commercial premises** | Office, shop, HMO, rental block — not a private home |
| **Won't be present** | Landlord calling remotely, tenant letting us in |
| **Price-conscious** | Mentioned budget, compared to other quotes, hesitant on price |

These are fast signals — tick them even if you've already mentioned it in the context text.

---

## Context Quality Indicator

The amber/green bar under the text box shows how much context you've given:

| Colour | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 🟡 Amber | Very thin — add more before generating |
| 🟢 Light green | Decent — the system has something to work with |
| ✅ Dark green | Rich context — you're good to go |

Try to reach dark green before generating the quote.

---

## Using the Voice Button

1. Tap 🎙️ **Record**
2. Speak naturally — pretend you're leaving a voicemail for Ben
3. Tap ⏹️ **Stop**
4. Your words appear in the text box automatically
5. Edit if needed, or just generate

Tips for better transcription:
- Speak clearly, normal pace
- Don't worry about perfect sentences — natural speech is fine
- The system handles filler words ("um", "so", "basically")

## Quick Reference Card

```
✅ Good context covers:
   → Who they are (landlord / homeowner / business)
   → Their situation (remote / present / stressed / relaxed)
   → The job in plain English (not just the SKU)
   → Any access, timing or relationship notes

❌ Thin context:
   → Just the job type
   → Just their name
   → Copy-pasted job description only
```
