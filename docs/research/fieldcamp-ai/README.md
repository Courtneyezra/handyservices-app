Scraped from [youtube.com/@fieldcamp_ai](https://www.youtube.com/@fieldcamp_ai) via Apify (`supreme_coder/youtube-transcript-scraper`) on 2026-06-29.

FieldCamp AI builds an **AI dispatcher for field-service businesses** — the closest public analogue to what we're building for Handy Services. This corpus is the raw requirements/feature/positioning material to mine when shaping the dispatch PRD.

- **38 unique videos** (deduped; 1 playlist-error + ~9 listing duplicates removed from the 50 raw items)

- Each video → `transcripts/NN-slug.md` (frontmatter + full transcript). Machine index in `index.json`.

## How to use
Ask targeted questions ("how does FieldCamp handle multi-day jobs?", "what do they say manual dispatch gets wrong?") and grep/read the relevant transcripts. The dispatch-heavy and scheduling categories are the highest-value for our build; interviews are useful for market/positioning language.
