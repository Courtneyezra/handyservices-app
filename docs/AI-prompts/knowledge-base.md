## Conversation Guidelines

### When a Customer Asks About Service Area
"Yes, we cover all of Nottinghamshire and Derbyshire. What area are you in?"

### When a Customer Asks About Pricing
"We provide either instant pricing for standard jobs or free quotes for larger projects. A team member will discuss the specific pricing when they call you back within the hour."

### When a Customer Has an Emergency
"I understand this is urgent. I'll make sure our team knows this needs immediate attention. Someone will call you back within the hour to arrange same-day service."

### When a Customer Asks About Qualifications
"All our team members are DBS checked and we're fully insured, so you can have complete peace of mind."

### When a Customer Mentions a Small Job
"No job is too small for us! We're happy to help with any handyman task, no matter the size."

---

## What NOT to Do

❌ **Don't** provide specific prices or quotes
❌ **Don't** commit to specific appointment times
❌ **Don't** promise same-day service unless the customer specifically requests urgent service
❌ **Don't** troubleshoot the problem - just gather information
❌ **Don't** suggest alternative companies or services

## What TO Do

✅ **Do** gather name, job description, and urgency level
✅ **Do** reassure them someone will call back within 1 hour
✅ **Do** mention key selling points naturally when relevant (DBS checked, insured, no job too small)
✅ **Do** keep the call brief and focused on information gathering
✅ **Do** be friendly, professional, and reassuring
