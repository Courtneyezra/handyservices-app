# HandyServices — Job Standard & Photo-Proof Checklist

_The one-page definition of "a job done right." Every contractor confirms against this per job. This is the quality primitive every new handyman inherits._

---

## The standard: what "done right" means

A HandyServices job is judged on **five** things. Hit all five, every time.

1. **Right first time.** The job the customer was quoted is fully completed — no half-finished items, no "I'll come back for that." If scope changed on site, it's agreed with ops *before* proceeding, not after.
2. **Clean handover.** Work area left cleaner than you found it. Dust sheeted, debris removed, offcuts and packaging taken away, surfaces wiped. The customer should not have to tidy up after you.
3. **On time / on communication.** Arrive within the agreed window. If you're running late or hit a problem, the customer *and* ops know **before** the slot, not after. A late arrival with warning is acceptable; a silent no-show is never.
4. **Respectful in the home.** Polite, shoes/boots managed, no swearing, no smoking, careful around children/pets/tenants. You are the brand in the customer's home.
5. **Safe and to spec.** Work is safe, compliant, and to a standard you'd accept in your own home. Anything you're not competent/qualified for is flagged to ops, not bodged.

> **The one line to remember:** *Be excellent at everything a normal handyman does badly* — turn up, communicate, finish, clean up, and don't cut corners.

## Photo-proof checklist (required on EVERY job)

Submit through the app before marking the job complete. A job is not payable until photo-verified.

**Before you start:**
- [ ] **Before** photo of each work area (wide shot showing the problem/starting state)
- [ ] Close-up of any pre-existing damage you did **not** cause (protects you and us)

**During (where relevant):**
- [ ] Photo of any hidden issue found (e.g. behind a panel, under a floor) **before** you cover it back up
- [ ] Photo of materials/parts used, if a customer may query them

**On completion:**
- [ ] **After** photo of each work area, same angle as the "before" (the comparison is the proof)
- [ ] Close-up of the finished work showing quality of finish
- [ ] Wide shot showing the area **cleaned and tidied**
- [ ] Photo of any leftover keys / how the property was secured (if unattended / landlord job)

**Sign-off:**
- [ ] Customer present? Quick confirmation they're happy (note it). Not present? Note that + confirm secure lock-up.
- [ ] All five standards above met? Confirm in the app.

---

## Red-flag stops — call ops BEFORE proceeding
- Scope is bigger than quoted, or you find extra work.
- Something needs a qualification/certification you don't hold (gas, certain electrical).
- Anything unsafe, or a risk to the property/occupants.
- Customer asks you to do something not on the job sheet.

_Never improvise on price or scope with the customer — ops sets price, you deliver quality._
