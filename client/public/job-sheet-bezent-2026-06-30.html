Re-seal the bath with new silicone

Strip out all the old silicone around the bath. Clean and fully dry the joint, then put on fresh sanitary silicone in a clean, even line.
⚠ Important Fill the bath with water before you seal it — the joint moves under the weight, so the seal stays good once it's emptied. Let it dry before it's used, and tell Tara how long to leave it.
2
Patch the hole in the kitchen ceiling
About 1 hour
Cut back to a firm edge, fit a plasterboard patch or scrim and filler depending on the size, fill it, then sand it back flush ready for paint.
⚠ Check first Look above the hole for pipes or cables before cutting. If there's any sign of a leak, take a photo and call Ben before you patch it.
