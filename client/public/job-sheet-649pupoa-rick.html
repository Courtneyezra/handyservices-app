Scope of Work

1. Paint three rooms — walls + ceilings
Three rooms: 4×3m, 2×2m, 2×1.5m. Walls and ceilings only. Prep, cut in, two coats emulsion. Cover floors and furniture, full clean-up after.
⚠ On-site: Confirm room sizes on arrival. Two coats minimum on every wall/ceiling — patchy single coats will trigger a callback.
2. Re-seal bath with new silicone
Strip existing silicone, clean and dry the joint, apply fresh sanitary-grade silicone around the bath. Tool to a clean bead.
⚠ On-site: Bath must be filled with water during application — empties later as silicone cures with the joint compressed.
3. Gutters — clear + replace corner piece
Clear all gutters and downpipes of debris on the property. Replace the broken/leaking gutter corner piece with a new fitting. Test water flow after.
⚠ On-site: Working at height — ladder must be footed or use a tower if conditions require. Photograph downpipe outflow after clearing as proof.
4. Replace bathroom extractor fan (CVT2 model)
Remove existing bathroom extractor. Install supplied CVT2 fan. Wire into existing isolator switch (no new circuit). Test for proper extraction.
⚠ On-site: Isolate at consumer unit before any wiring work. If existing wiring is unsuitable (no isolator, undersized cable) STOP and call Ben — do not bodge.
5. Add 2 ventilation roof tiles
Install 2 vent tiles into existing roof to support extractor outflow. Slate-rip, slot in vent tile, weather-proof. Match existing roof material.
⚠ On-site: Working at height — full safety. Photograph slate alignment before lifting and after install. If felt underneath is damaged, flag for separate repair.
